Csicsergő-félsziget

The Csicsergő-félsziget is a quiet, nature-rich area in Balatonberény, known for its lush vegetation and birdlife. A lookout tower offers a panoramic view of Lake Balaton and the surrounding hills.

Somoshegy kilátó (Lookout tower)

A wooden, multi-level observation tower located at the edge of the Somos forest near Balatonberény. From the top, you get a stunning view of the western basin of Lake Balaton and the surrounding hills. It's easily accessible and perfect for short hikes or photography.

Get Directions
Kőtenger (Stonesea)

Kőtenger is one of the natural wonders of the Balaton Uplands, located in the Káli Basin. This area features strange-shaped sandstone boulders, forming a maze-like rock field ideal for walks and exploring unique natural formations.
