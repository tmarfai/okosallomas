Gömbkilátó Lookout

This iconic spherical lookout tower stands atop a hill. Originally built for the 1958 Brussels World Expo, it was moved here in the 1960s. With a diameter of 15m made of 240 triangles, it offers a panoramic view of Lake Balaton from 165m above sea level – especially stunning when lit up at sunset. Nearby, there’s an adventure park, alpine coaster, and treetop trail for families.

Wine & Gastronomy

Balatonboglár is exceptional in its wines: Chardonnay, Olaszrizling, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Noir and more are grown here — the wine region covers over 3,200 hectares! It's also a base for Champagne production. Local wineries offer tastings and tours (e.g. Légli, Szentpály cellars), and the famous Boglári Harvest Festival is held annually.

Lake & Boating Experience

The town’s port hosts the Lake Balaton swim and boating events – sailboats and excursion boats depart from here. The historic steamship "Csavargőzös" preserves the history of navigation and offers guided historic cruises.
