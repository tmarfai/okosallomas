Other destinations nearby

Balatonfenyves Beach

A long, well-maintained shoreline with shallow water and a beautiful panorama of Lake Balaton. Free entry, shaded trees, playground, snack bars, and pedal boat rentals await visitors.

Get Directions
Balatonfenyvesi kisvasút (Narrow gauge Railway)

Hungary's only scheduled narrow gauge railway still in operation. It runs on two lines: to Imremajor and Pálmajor, through beautiful marshland scenery.

Get Directions
Badacsony

Take the ferry from Fonyód to easily reach the northern shore of Lake Balaton and Badacsony. Famous for its basalt columns, wine cellars, and scenic views.
