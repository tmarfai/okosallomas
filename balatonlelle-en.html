Amusement Park

Balatonlelle’s amusement park is a true family entertainment hub, offering fun for all ages. Whether you're looking for thrilling roller coasters, cheerful carousels, or interactive games, the park guarantees enjoyment during summer days.

Lelle Eye

Located in the heart of Balatonlelle near the Móló Promenade, the Lelle Eye is a 30-meter-tall Ferris wheel that has been welcoming visitors since summer 2020. The three-round ride offers a stunning view: the entire lake surface, the marina, and even the distant hills including the iconic witness hills.

Gömbkilátó (Globe Lookout Tower)

This iconic spherical lookout tower stands atop a hill. Originally built for the 1958 Brussels World Expo, it was moved here in the 1960s. With a diameter of 15m made of 240 triangles, it offers a panoramic view of Lake Balaton from 165m above sea level – especially stunning when lit up at sunset. Nearby, there’s an adventure park, alpine coaster, and treetop trail for families.
