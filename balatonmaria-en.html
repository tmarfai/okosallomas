Balatonmáriafürdő Harbor

The harbor of Balatonmáriafürdő is one of the town's most popular spots, where nature meets the tranquil atmosphere of Lake Balaton. From the pier, you get a stunning view of the lake's widest part — especially magical at sunset.

Nagyboldogasszony-kápolna (Assumption Chapel)

Located in the heart of Balatonmáriafürdő, this charming little chapel offers a peaceful spiritual retreat. Its unique charm comes from the lush trees, the well-kept garden, and the quaint little bell tower standing beside it.

Buffalo Reserve

The Buffalo Reserve in Kápolnapuszta is a truly unique destination just 20 minutes from Balatonmáriafürdő. Here you can see Hungary's largest buffalo herd up close while walking along an educational trail that introduces the wildlife of the Little Balaton.
