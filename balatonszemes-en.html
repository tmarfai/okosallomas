Post Museum

The Post Museum in Balatonszemes offers a unique insight into the history of postal transportation, from the Middle Ages to the present day. The exhibition, titled “From Cart to Electric Vehicle,” showcases the evolution of transport vehicles used in postal services.

Lelle Eye

Located in the heart of Balatonlelle near the Móló promenade, the Lelle Eye is a 30-meter-high Ferris wheel that has welcomed visitors since the summer of 2020. During the three-round ride, you can enjoy a breathtaking panorama: the full surface of Lake Balaton, the harbor, and the hills on the northern shore including the famous witness hills.

Sphere Lookout

The iconic sphere lookout on the hilltop was originally created for the 1958 Brussels World’s Fair and was later relocated here in the 1960s. With a diameter of 15 meters made of 240 triangles, it provides a wide view of Lake Balaton from a height of 165 meters – especially stunning when illuminated at sunset. Nearby you'll find an adventure park, alpine coaster, and treetop walkway for families.
