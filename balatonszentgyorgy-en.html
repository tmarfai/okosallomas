Csillagvár Museum

Csillagvár is unique both in form and content: this 19th-century hunting lodge features period furnishings, life-sized dioramas, and a hunting history exhibition. Kids will enjoy a petting zoo and playground, while adults can glimpse into the life of Hungarian nobility – all in a scenic setting just minutes from Lake Balaton...

Festetics Palace

Close to the shores of Lake Balaton stands a stunning monument of elegance and history where each room tells a story. The Festetics Palace is more than a baroque building – it's a former noble residence with a grand library, carriage collection, luxurious rooms, and an English-style garden. The Helikon Library, Hunting and Carriage Museums are each worth a visit, and together they offer a full day of discovery for those who appreciate the charm, beauty, and peace of the past...

Gömbkilátó (Globe Lookout Tower)

This iconic spherical lookout tower stands atop a hill. Originally built for the 1958 Brussels World Expo, it was moved here in the 1960s. With a diameter of 15m made of 240 triangles, it offers a panoramic view of Lake Balaton from 165m above sea level – especially stunning when lit up at sunset. Nearby, there’s an adventure park, alpine coaster, and treetop trail for families.
