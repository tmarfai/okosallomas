Lake Szállásréti

Located near Dombóvár, Lake Szállásréti offers peace and relaxation along the Kapos‑hegyháti Nature Park and the valley of Kis‑Konda Stream. Along the lake shore, you'll find a picturesque promenade, benches, unique sculptures, a Kneipp trail, and an outdoor fitness park.
