Lake Gyékényes

The Kotró, also known as the Gyékényes quarry lake, with its crystal-clear, deep waters, is not only a favorite among summer swimmers but also a true paradise for anglers. The lake's surroundings are peaceful and calm, making it the perfect spot for relaxation—whether it's sunbathing, camping, or an evening boat ride.
