Abalee-get railway station is located in Baranha County, in the village of Abalee-get. It is operated by the Hungarian State Railways and is part of railway line number 40, connecting Budapest–Dombóvár–Pécs. The station is small and simple in design, with passenger trains regularly stopping there. Although traffic is now moderate, the stop is important for tourists, as it provides access to the Abalee-get Cave and the Bat Museum.
