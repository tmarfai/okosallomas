Balatonmáriafürdő railway station is located at the western end of the town, close to the railway crossing of Route 7119. The station is part of the Budapest–Murakeresztúr main line, railway line number 30, providing frequent and direct connections to the settlements along the southern shore of Lake Balaton as well as to more distant parts of the country. The building features the historicist style typical of the Hungarian State Railways stations around Lake Balaton; it was renovated and made accessible in 2023, with a refurbished waiting room, a modern ticket office, and new restrooms welcoming passengers. The station has a staffed ticket office, and nearby parking and bicycle storage facilities make it a convenient starting point for visiting the beaches and exploring the town.
