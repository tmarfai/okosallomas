Balatonmáriafürdő railway station is located at the western end of the village, close to the railway crossing of Main Road 7119. The station lies on the Budapest–Murakeresztúr main line, also known as Line 30, providing frequent and direct connections along the southern shore of Lake Balaton and to more distant parts of Hungary. The building reflects the historic architectural style typical of Balaton railway stations; it was renovated and made fully accessible in 2023, with a refurbished waiting room, modern ticket office and new restrooms. The station also offers a staffed ticket desk, parking facilities and bicycle storage, making it a convenient starting point for reaching the local beaches and exploring the town
