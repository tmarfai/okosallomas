Shomodgjsob railway station is located in Shomodgj County and operated by the Hungarian State Railways.
It is a junction station, with trains departing in four directions: towards Ghaicainnash–Nadj-kha-neee-zha, Kaposhvár–Dombóvár, Martzali–Balatonsendyurj, and Nadjatád. The latter railway line once extended all the way to Bartch.
