Upside Down House

At the Upside Down House in Siófok, everything is literally turned on its head. As you walk in, you’ll feel the strange sensation of seeing furniture hanging from the ceiling and the floor above your head. This unusual attraction not only provides hilarious photo opportunities but also brings a smile to every visitor’s face.
