Busójárás

Busójárás is a unique, winter-chasing folk tradition in Mohács, held every year at the end of the carnival, on Shrove Tuesday. The main characters, the busós, wear wooden carved, frightening masks and fur coats, parading with rattles, cowbells, and noise-making devices to chase away winter and evil spirits.

Danube Riverside

The Danube riverside in Mohács is one of the city’s most beautiful and peaceful areas, where nature, history, and relaxation meet. A long promenade runs along the river, offering a beautiful view of the Danube and the opposite shore. Benches, green areas, and shady trees make it a pleasant resting place.

Duna-Dráva National Park

The Duna–Dráva National Park is a wild, natural treasure in Southern Transdanubia, around Mohács, with floodplains, backwaters, and gallery forests.
