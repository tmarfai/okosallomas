Useful information about the station

Address

Railway station: Indóház tér 1.
Bus station: Nagy Lajos király útja 20.

Ticket Office and Tickets

Railway ticket office: Mon-Sun 04:20-21:50
International ticket office: Mon-Sun 08:10-19:30
Ticketing: machine-issued tickets and ticket vending machine.

Waiting Room and Restrooms

Waiting room: daily 03:45-23:15
Restrooms and luggage lockers are available at the station.

Accessibility

Lift assistance is always available at the station.
Bus station: guiding strip, audio passenger information and induction loop.

Contact

Railway customer service: Mon-Sun and holidays 07:00-19:00; breaks 11:20-11:40 and 15:20-15:45.
Bus station customer service: weekdays 07:00-15:00.
MÁVDIREKT: +36 (1) 3 49 49 49; Mobile: +36 (20/30/70) 499 4999.

Additional Services

A bus ticket office is available at the bus station.
Parking and bicycle parking are available near the station.
