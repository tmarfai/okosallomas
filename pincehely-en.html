Roman Catholic church

The Roman Catholic church of Pincehely is one of the village’s most significant monuments and historic buildings, located prominently in the village center. Its architecture reflects both rural simplicity and a deep respect for religious traditions, which form an important part of the community’s life.

Pipó Castle of Ozora

Ozora Castle is one of the most beautiful and interesting historical sights in Tolna County, located just a few kilometers from Pincehely. It was built in the early 15th century by Pipó of Ozora, a loyal commander of King Sigismund, who brought the Renaissance architectural style from Italy—making this castle the first secular Renaissance palace in Hungary.

Tamási Forest Park

The Tamási Forest Park and Wildlife Park, also known as Miklósvár Forest Park, is an excellent destination if you want to explore nature, forest trails, and native big game animals around the Pincehely area.
