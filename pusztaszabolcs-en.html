Velence Lake

Lake Velence is one of Hungary’s most popular holiday destinations, located just about half an hour from Pusztaszabolcs. Its special feature is that, due to its shallow water, it warms up quickly, making it ideal for summer swimming. Along the lake’s shore, there are several developed beaches, bike paths, and nature reserves, offering suitable activities for those who want to relax, exercise, or hike.

Székesfehérvár

Székesfehérvár is one of Hungary’s oldest and most historically significant cities. The former coronation city attracts visitors with its rich history, historic monuments, and charming downtown area.

Brunszvik Palace

The Brunszvik Palace attracts visitors with its romantic atmosphere and rich plant life. A special feature of the palace is that Beethoven visited it several times, and his memory is preserved in a museum.
