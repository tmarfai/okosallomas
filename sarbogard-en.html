Why should you visit Sárbogárd?

Sárbogárd is one of the most important railway junctions in Transdanubia, where lines from three directions
meet - making it an excellent spot not only for transfers but also for railway enthusiasts to explore and discover.

Simontornya Castle

Built in the 1270s, the castle has functioned as a museum since 1975. Permanent exhibitions include: History of the Castle, Gallery of Tolna County Nobility, 16th-century coin hoard, and paintings by artist Arany Bazsonyi.
