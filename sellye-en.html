City Thermal Bath

Located on the northern edge of Sellye, the bath is set in a beautifully landscaped 3.2-hectare area. The beach, with a capacity of 1,100 people, features swimming, training, thermal sitting, and children's pools. The thermal water emerges from a depth of 800 meters at a temperature of 48°C.

Ős-Dráva Visitor Center

The Ős-Dráva Visitor Center introduces the natural values and traditions of the Ormánság region through an interactive exhibition, a display of indigenous domestic animals, nature trails, diverse active programs, and environmental education activities.

Draskovich Castle

The castle was originally built in Baroque style by the Batthyány family and took its current form in the mid-18th century. In the early 1800s, both the castle and its surrounding lands became the property of the Draskovich family as a wedding gift when Károly Draskovich married Erzsébet Batthyány-Strattmann.
