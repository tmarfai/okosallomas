Why should you visit Simontornya?

Simontornya Castle

Built in the 1270s, the castle has functioned as a museum since 1975. Permanent exhibitions include: Castle history, Gallery of the Nobility of Tolna County, 16th-century coin hoard of Simontornya, and artworks by painter Arany Bazsonyi.

Roman Catholic Church

This single-nave church has a tower built into its main façade. The entrance opens through a column-framed stone portal topped with a cornice and decorative vase, bearing the date 1760. Its sanctuary is narrower than the nave. A built-in organ loft is located above the entrance. The interior furnishings mostly date from the late 18th century.

Aranyponty Fishing Museum

The Aranyponty Fishing Museum is a perfect choice for an exciting cultural adventure: authentic artifacts, touching stories, and photographs – all deeply connected to nature and our traditions. A special experience for children, families, and anyone interested in the rich heritage of fishing.
