Upside-down House

At the Siófok Upside-down House, everything is literally turned upside down. Upon entering, you immediately get a strange feeling as the furniture hangs from the ceiling and the floor is above your head. This unusual sight not only offers fun photo opportunities but also brings a smile to every visitor's face.

Bella Animal Park

The Siófok Bella Animal Park is a friendly, family-oriented place where you can meet llamas, ponies, and meerkats up close. Children especially love camel and pony rides, as well as watching capybaras bathing in the small pond. The park invites a pleasant walk and is a great program for a relaxing afternoon near Lake Balaton.
