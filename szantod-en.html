Ferry Chapel

Nestled quietly at the Szántód ferry shore stands the Ferry Chapel, symbolizing the traditions connected to Lake Balaton and a place of spiritual peace. This small white chapel is modest but has a special atmosphere – it's worth stopping here for a moment while waiting for the ferry or taking a stroll by the water. A perfect spot to reflect away from the hustle and bustle of the lake...

Szántód Ferry Port

The most iconic spot in Szántód is the ferry crossing, offering spectacular views of the Tihany Peninsula. The ferry is not only a useful transport option but also a classic Balaton experience — accessible by bike, on foot, or by car.
