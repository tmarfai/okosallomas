Other destinations nearby

Wine Museum

An interactive museum set up in a medieval building where you can explore the professional and cultural values of the local wine region through the stories of Szekszárd’s Kadarka, Kékfrankos and Bikavér wines.

Get Directions
Kálvária Lookout

The Kálvária Lookout is one of Szekszárd’s most famous sights, located at the top of Bartina Hill. It offers a stunning panoramic view of the city, the Szekszárd Hills, and the surrounding vineyards. A walking trail leads up to it, making it a perfect spot for a short hike.

Get Directions
Augusz House

The Augusz House is one of Szekszárd’s most beautiful and well-known buildings, showcasing three generations of historical development. For those who appreciate architectural, cultural, and musical heritage, it’s worth seeing from the outside – the cozy courtyard and elegant façade are a joy to behold.
