Szigetvár Castle

Not only the castle but also the city’s most significant historical memory is the 1566 siege. Sultan Suleiman I camped with his hundred-thousand-strong army on the eastern side of the castle and called for Zrínyi’s surrender. Zrínyi refused, and the siege began, during which the Ottoman army’s numbers dwindled, but the defenders of the castle also suffered heavy losses.

Szigetvár Thermal Bath

One of Szigetvár’s most popular places is the Szigetvár Thermal Bath, known for its excellent quality medicinal water. It offers refreshing relaxation opportunities for every family member throughout the year.

Ökopark - Bükkösd

A great excursion spot for kindergarten and school groups. A huge playground, an experience barn with folk games, and a barefoot trail await both young and old. Visitors can try horseback riding, carriage rides, compete in relay races, sleep in yurts, and hike through the forest at night.
